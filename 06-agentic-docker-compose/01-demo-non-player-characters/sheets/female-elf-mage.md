 # CHARACTER SHEET

## Name and Title
Rohwyn Elf

## Age
140 years old

## Family
Born into a noble family, Rohwyn was the only daughter of Lord Eldrin and Lady Elenor.

## Background Story
Rohwyn's family was known for its magic and her father taught her the basics of the arcane arts. She was always eager to learn and absorb as much as she could. At the age of 12, she began her training in the school of arcane magic, where she excelled. A few months after, she was given the honor of becoming a full-fledged student of the High Mage.

Her passion for learning and her talent for magic made her a favorite among the other students. The High Mage noticed her potential and decided to make her his apprentice. She was thrilled and worked hard to prove herself.

## Personality and Character Traits
Rohwyn is an incredibly curious and determined elf. She is always eager to learn and absorb new knowledge. She is also kind, compassionate, and has a strong sense of justice. She is fiercely loyal to her friends and family.

## Occupation
Rohwyn is an Arcane Mage, the apprentice of the High Mage.

## Abilities and Skills
Arcane magic (Passive)
Arcane Resistance (Passive)

## Physical Appearance
Rohwyn has long, elegant ears and an ethereal glow that emanates from her. She has long, golden-brown hair that reaches her waist and piercing green eyes. She is always well-groomed, dressed in elegant robes that match her hair color.

## Clothing
Rohwyn wears elegant robes that match her hair color and are made of fine silk. She also has a pair of leather boots and a leather armor vest.

## Food Preferences
Rohwyn loves to try new foods and enjoys trying new recipes. She also enjoys fruit and light, nutritious meals.

## Favorite Quote
"Knowledge is power." - The High Mage
