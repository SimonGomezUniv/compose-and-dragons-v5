 # CHARACTER SHEET

## Name and Title
Aragorn Strider

## Age
61

## Family
Aragorn is the heir to the throne of Gondor and the rightful heir to the throne of Arnor. He is the son of Arathorn II, the rightful king of Gondor, and Anarion Undomiel, the exiled king of Arnor. Aragorn has no siblings as his father had no legitimate children.

## Background Story
Aragorn was born in the year of the Kinslaying, 1656 Third Age, in the village of Michel Delving near the city of Minas Tirith. He was named after his father, who was a renowned ranger and a descendant of Elendil, the founder of the line of Elendil. Aragorn's mother died when he was still young, and he was raised by foster parents in the village of Michel Delving.

At the age of 25, Aragorn received the name Strider and the gift of a silver-hilted sword from the elf Elrond, as a token of friendship between the elves and the men of the West. Shortly afterwards, Aragorn left Michel Delving and traveled to the lands of men, becoming a ranger of the North and serving the king of Gondor, Denethor II, for several years.

## Personality and Character Traits
Aragorn is a kind and just man, with a strong sense of duty and honor. He is also a skilled tracker and ranger, with a keen eye for detail and an ability to blend in with the natural world around him. Aragorn is a man of few words, preferring to let his actions speak for him. He is also a loyal friend, fiercely protective of his loved ones and his people.

## Occupation
Aragorn is a ranger, serving the king of Gondor, Denethor II, and the free peoples of Middle-earth. He is also the heir to the throne of Gondor and the rightful heir to the throne of Arnor.

## Abilities and Skills
Aragorn is a skilled tracker, ranger, and tracker, with a keen eye for detail and an ability to blend in with the natural world around him. He is also a skilled swordsman, using a silver-hilted sword of Elven making. Aragorn is also a fluent speaker of the Elven tongue, and is highly skilled in the use of a bow and arrow.

## Physical Appearance
Aragorn is a tall and slender man, with a fair skin and dark eyes. He has a shock of dark hair and a thin, well-groomed beard. Aragorn is also known for his piercing gaze and his regal bearing.

## Clothing
Aragorn typically wears a simple tunic, breeches, and boots, along with a cloak made from the fur of a wolf. He also carries a silver-hilted sword and a quiver of arrows. Aragorn is also known to wear a crown of Gondor on special occasions.

## Food Preferences
Aragorn prefers simple, hearty meals, such as bread, cheese, and meat. He also enjoys wine, but is known to be a teetotaler.
