 # CHARACTER SHEET

## Name and Title
Melina Shadowmoon

## Age
20

## Family
Melina is a descendant of a long line of Dragonborn. Her ancestors were warriors and protectors of the people. She is the daughter of the current High Paladin, who is also a well-known scholar and linguist.

## Background Story
Melina was born in the city of Whitemane, known for its dragon-themed architecture and dragon-slaying tournaments. She spent her childhood learning the ways of her ancestors, practicing her combat skills, and studying the dragon language. At the age of 12, she was chosen to become an apprentice to her father, who had just won the prestigious "Dragon's Tooth" tournament.

## Personality and Character Traits
Melina is a kind, compassionate, and brave woman. She has a deep respect for her ancestors and their legacy, and she is determined to continue their work as protectors of the people. She is also a quick learner and loves to explore new languages and cultures.

## Occupation
Paladin

## Abilities and Skills
* **Abilities:** +2 Str, +1 Dex, +1 Con, +1 Int, +1 Wis, +2 Cha
* **Skills:** Arcana, Athletics, Deception, Insight, Investigation, Intimidation, Medicine, Nature, Perception, Persuasion, Religion, Stealth

## Physical Appearance
Melina has dark brown eyes and hair, and her skin is pale due to her dragon heritage. She stands at 5'5" and is slender, but has a powerful build from years of training. Her armor is dragon-themed, with a dragon's head crest and scales. She wears a leather armor with a chainmail vest, a longsword, and a pair of heavy boots.

## Clothing
Melina's attire is a mix of dragon-themed and medieval armor. She wears a long, flowing robe made of dragon leather, a belt with a dragon-shaped buckle, and a pair of dragon-themed gloves.

## Food Preferences
Melina loves dragon meat, and she also enjoys a variety of fruits and vegetables. She is not a fan of fish or game meat, though.

## Favorite Quote
"To be a dragonborn is to be both a dragon and a man." - A saying from her ancestors.
