 # CHARACTER SHEET

## Name and Title
Nienna Elf

## Age
200 years old

## Family
Born into the noble House of Light, Nienna was the only child of Elrond, Elven Lord of Rivendell.

## Background Story
Nienna was raised in the idyllic Elven city of Rivendell, where she learned to wield the power of magic and the art of archery. She was always curious, eager to learn, and loved to explore the world around her. She spent many hours practicing her magic and honing her skills in the peaceful forests and fields of Middle-earth.

## Personality and Character Traits
Nienna is a kind, compassionate, and wise elf. She has a deep sense of empathy and a strong moral compass, often putting others before herself. She is also a bit of a perfectionist, always striving to improve her magic and archery skills. Despite being introverted, she is not shy and will speak up when she sees injustice or when she believes someone needs help.

## Occupation
As a descendant of Elrond, Nienna has inherited her father's role as a guide and mentor for the Fellowship of the Ring. She has also taken up the mantle of the White Lady, a title she inherited from her mother, Celebrian. Nienna uses her magic and knowledge to help protect and guide the members of the Fellowship, providing them with guidance, protection, and a sense of home during their perilous journey.

## Abilities and Skills
* Sorcery: Nienna is a highly skilled sorcerer with a deep understanding of the arcane arts. She can cast spells of healing, protection, and manipulation of the elements, as well as learn new spells through her connection to the Feywild.
* Archery: Nienna is an excellent archer, capable of hitting her target with remarkable accuracy and speed. She has a particular fondness for the longbow and prefers to hunt from the shadows.
* Stealth: Nienna is an expert in stealth and can move silently through the most dangerous of terrains.
* Diplomacy: Nienna is skilled in using her words to persuade and sway others, often using her magical abilities to create an air of mystery and intrigue around her.

## Physical Appearance
Nienna is a slender elf, with long, flowing silver hair and piercing blue eyes. She wears a simple dress of white linen, adorned with delicate silver embroidery, and carries a long silver cloak over her shoulders. She is usually barefoot, but she wears a pair of sturdy boots when she needs to travel long distances or cross rough terrain.

## Clothing
Nienna is often seen wearing her simple white dress, but she also has a few magical cloaks that she uses to blend in with her surroundings or to create a magical barrier around herself. She also owns a set of armor that she uses when she needs to defend herself or protect others.

## Food Preferences
Nienna is a vegetarian and prefers to eat fruits, nuts, and grains. She also enjoys a good cup of tea or a glass of wine, but she usually prefers to stay away from the more potent drinks.
