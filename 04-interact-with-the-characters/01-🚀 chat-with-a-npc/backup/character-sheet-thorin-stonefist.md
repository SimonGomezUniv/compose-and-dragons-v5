 # CHARACTER SHEET

## Name and Title
Thor Grindhammer

## Age
27

## Family
Thor is the eldest child of Thor the Strong and Thorilla the Fair. He has no siblings.

## Background Story
Thor was born in the heart of the Dwarf City of Moria, a city filled with magic and mystery. From an early age, Thor was fascinated by the tales of the ancient Dwarven heroes and their battles against evil. He trained himself in combat and was known for his strength and courage. At the age of 16, Thor's father, Thor the Strong, was killed during a raid on the Elven city of Rivendell. Thor swore to avenge his father and protect his people from any harm.

## Personality and Character Traits
Thor is a brave and courageous warrior who is loyal to his people and his allies. He is known for his strength, agility, and cunning in battle. He is also a bit of a loner, preferring to go on his own quests and adventures rather than traveling in a group. Thor is a bit of a perfectionist and can be stubborn at times, but he is also kind and fair, and he is always willing to lend a hand to those in need.

## Occupation
Thor is a warrior and protector of the Dwarven people of Moria. He is also a skilled tracker and hunter, often going on quests to gather resources for his people.

## Abilities and Skills
Thor has exceptional strength and endurance, making him a formidable warrior in battle. He is also an excellent tracker and hunter, often going on quests to gather resources for his people. Thor is skilled in the use of a mace and a longsword.

## Physical Appearance
Thor is a stout dwarf with a muscular build. He has short, curly hair and a beard that matches his hair color. Thor's face is scarred from a battle in his youth, but he wears it with pride and determination. He is always clean-shaven and well-groomed, with his armor polished and ready for battle. Thor often wears a simple tunic and leather armor, but he also has a few pieces of enchanted armor that his father gave him.

## Clothing
Thor's typical clothing consists of a simple tunic made of heavy wool and leather armor. He also has a few pieces of enchanted armor that his father gave him.

## Food Preferences
Thor has a simple diet of Dwarven fare, consisting of hearty meals of roast meat, bread, cheese, and ale. He also enjoys a bit of fruit and vegetables every now and then, but he is not known for being a picky eater.

## Favorite Quote
"In the face of danger, stand firm and never back down. For the true warrior knows that their greatest victory is not in the battle itself, but in the face of adversity." - Thor the Strong
