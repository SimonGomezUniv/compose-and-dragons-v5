### Clan Names
- Ironbeard, Stonefist, Forgeheart, Goldhammer, Axehand, Firebeard, Blazeforge, Bronzeshield, Mountainfoot, Rockbreaker

### Generation Rules
- Favor hard consonants (k, t, d, g)
- Use short, punchy sounds
- Incorporate references to metals, stones, forging
- Common suffixes: -in, -or, -ar, -im
- Typical classes: Warrior, Cleric, Paladin, Berserker

### House Names
- Goldleaf, Evenstar, Woodwalker, Moonsong, Dawnlight, Windancer, Treevoice, Shadowhunter, Stargazer, Lightbow

### Generation Rules
- Favor fluid consonants (l, n, r)
- Use many vowels
- Incorporate nature and star references
- Names typically long and melodious
- Common prefixes: El-, Cel-, Gal-
- Common suffixes: -il, -iel, -or, -ion
- Typical classes: Ranger, Mage, Druid, Bard, Rogue

- Strider, Towerguard, Stronghand, Rider, Steelblade, Proudheart, Valor, Citywatch, Spearlord, Windwalker

### Generation Rules
- Greater variety of sounds
- Mix of short and long names
- Can borrow elements from other races
- Family names often descriptive or location-based
- Common suffixes: -or, -wyn, -iel
- Common prefixes: Theo-, El-, Ar-
- Typical classes: Warrior, Paladin, Cleric, Rogue, Fighter, Monk
