## Family

Nienna is the last of her line, the Radiant Sorceress of the Silver Elves. Her ancestors were among the most powerful and revered sorcerers, healers, and shamans in the history of the Silver Elves. She is the last remaining descendant of the Aredhel, the family that bore the Heart of the Silver Elves.

As she grew older, Nienna began to study the ways of the Silver Elves, learning the secrets of the ancient magic and the ways of the Silver Elven shamans.
