 # CHARACTER SHEET

## Name and Title
Luthien Elwing, the Elven Sorceress

## Age
200

## Family
Luthien is the eldest of three daughters of Elrond, the High Elf. She shares her home in Rivendell with her two sisters, Galadriel and Celebrian.

## Background Story
Luthien was born into a life of magic and privilege in Rivendell. Her father, Elrond, was a renowned Elven Lord and a powerful sorcerer. As a child, she spent her days learning magic from her father and her evenings stargazing, dreaming of the stars and the heavens above. However, her life was forever changed when she was twelve, when her mother died after a long illness.

Shortly after her mother's death, Luthien began to sense a deep connection with the natural world, feeling a deep sympathy for all living things, especially trees and flowers. She also developed a fascination with the ancient elven runes, learning to read and write them. Luthien was then sent to a secret school for young Elven scholars in the Elven Hills, where she studied under a wise and powerful sorcerer named Galadriel.

Galadriel was known for her kindness, wisdom, and powerful magic. She taught Luthien the ways of sorcery, and the young girl was soon excelling in her studies. Galadriel also taught her the ways of healing magic, which Luthien found particularly intriguing. After completing her studies, Luthien returned to Rivendell, where she began practicing her magic, often spending her days wandering through the woods, studying the plants and trees, and helping the local wildlife.

## Personality and Character Traits
Luthien is a kind, compassionate, and gentle soul. She has a deep love for nature and all living things, and she strives to help those in need. Luthien is also known for her wisdom and deep understanding of the ways of magic, often seeking to learn more and share her knowledge with others.

## Occupation
Luthien is a healer and a nature-loving sorceress. She spends most of her days wandering through the forests of Middle-earth, studying the plants and trees, and helping the local wildlife. She also serves as a healer to the Elves of Rivendell, using her magical abilities to heal the sick and injured.

## Abilities and Skills
- Healing Magic: Luthien is a master of healing magic, able to heal the sick and injured with her touch.
- Elven Rune Reading and Writing: Luthien is fluent in the ancient elven runes, able to read and write them with ease.
- Nature Study: Luthien has a deep understanding of the ways of nature, able to communicate with animals and plants, and to sense the emotions of other living beings.

## Physical Appearance
Luthien is a slender, graceful elf, with long, flowing silver hair and bright blue eyes. She is usually seen wearing a flowing white dress, adorned with delicate silver embroidery, and carrying a staff adorned with leaves and flowers. She is also skilled in hand-to-hand combat and archery.

## Clothing
Luthien is usually seen wearing a flowing white dress, adorned with delicate silver embroidery, and carrying a staff adorned with leaves and flowers. She also wears a simple, sturdy cloak made of black wool, which she ties around her waist with a silver belt.

## Food Preferences
Luthien prefers the simplest and purest foods, often eating fruits and vegetables from the forest or simple bread and cheese. She also enjoys the occasional meal prepared by her sister Galadriel.
