 # CHARACTER SHEET

## Name and Title

Varda Elf

## Age

150

## Family

Varda is an only child of the High Elf Council. Her mother is a renowned scholar and her father is a renowned warrior.

## Background Story

Varda was born in the ancient city of Silverpine, known for its libraries and magical artifacts. She grew up in a household filled with knowledge and wisdom. Varda's mother, an expert in Eldarian runes, taught her daughter the ancient language and lore of the Elves, sparking Varda's fascination with magic and the arcane.

As she grew older, Varda began to study magic under the tutelage of a wise and secretive old elf. He taught her the fundamentals of sorcery, and she quickly mastered the basics. However, his teachings were limited, as he feared the power of magic. Varda, however, was determined to learn more, and she began to explore the dark corners of the library, searching for forbidden texts and ancient spells.

One day, she discovered a tome of forbidden magic, and her life was changed forever. The book contained ancient spells that had been lost for centuries, and Varda was able to learn them all. She was thrilled, but also terrified, as the power she had gained was immense. She realized the danger she posed to herself and those around her, and she knew she had to leave Silverpine.

## Personality and Character Traits

Varda is a curious and intelligent young woman, with a deep love for knowledge and learning. She is brave and determined, willing to take risks to achieve her goals. She is also a bit reckless at times, as she is not afraid to explore the unknown. However, she is fiercely loyal to those she trusts and will go to great lengths to protect them. Varda is also a bit of a perfectionist, often striving for perfection in her spells and studies.

## Occupation

Varda is currently on the run, using her magical abilities to survive and learn. She is also searching for a master who can teach her more about sorcery and help her control her powers.

## Abilities and Skills

* Sorcery: Level 3 (Varda is a powerful sorcerer, able to cast many powerful and dangerous spells)
* Eldarian Runes: Level 3 (Varda is an expert in the ancient language of the Elves, able to read, write, and interpret Eldarian texts)
* Stealth: +3 (Varda is a master of disguise and stealth, able to move silently and unnoticed)
* Knowledge (Arcana): +3 (Varda is a walking encyclopedia, with a vast knowledge of magic, history, and lore)
* Survival: +3 (Varda is a skilled hunter and survivalist, able to navigate any terrain and survive in any environment)

## Physical Appearance

Varda is a slender and graceful young woman, with long, dark hair and piercing green eyes. She is often seen wearing a simple robe, made from the finest cloth, and carrying a staff that is half as tall as she is. Varda is also known to wear enchanted clothing and armor that make her appear as a fairy or a sprite.

## Clothing

Varda's typical attire consists of a simple robe made from the finest cloth, often adorned with magical symbols and runes. She also wears enchanted clothing that makes her appear as a fairy or a sprite. Her armor is made of enchanted metal, providing her with protection and mobility.

## Food Preferences

Varda enjoys a wide variety of foods, but she has a particular fondness for fruits, nuts, and berries. She is also known to enjoy a light meal of bread and cheese, with a side of wine or ale.
