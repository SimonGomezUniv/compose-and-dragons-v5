 # CHARACTER SHEET

## Name and Title
Elara Elf

## Age
150

## Family

Born to a family of ancient elven scholars.

## Background Story

A young elf with a thirst for knowledge and adventure, Elara was always drawn to stories of heroes and sorcerers. After her father passed, she was left to her own devices to seek out her own path. With a bit of luck and the right connections, she was able to secure an apprenticeship with a local sorcerer. There she learned the art of casting spells, but always felt the pull of the wilderness.

## Personality and Character Traits

Elara is a curious and adventurous elf. She has a strong sense of justice and often puts herself in harm's way to seek out the truth. Elara is also a bit of a show off, and often finds herself in the middle of the attention of others.

## Occupation

Sorcerer, adventuring.

## Abilities and Skills

* Spellcasting (Sorcerer)
* Wilderness Survival (Elf)
* Knowledge (Elven Heritage)
* Insight (Elven Heritage)
* Deception (Sorcerer)

## Physical Appearance

A young elf with a slender frame and pointed ears. She has long, dark hair that she often ties back into a ponytail. Elara has piercing, bright blue eyes that seem to search for answers.

## Clothing

Typically wears a simple green tunic, dark blue pants, and green boots. She also carries a small backpack filled with various herbs and potions.

## Food Preferences

Elara is a bit of a vegetarian, preferring fruits, vegetables, and nuts. She also enjoys a good ale every now and then.

## Favorite Quote

"The only thing necessary for the triumph of evil is that good men do nothing." - Edmund Burke

## A Secret Word

Fire.
