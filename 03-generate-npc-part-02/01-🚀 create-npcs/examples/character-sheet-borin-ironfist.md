# CHARACTER SHEET

## Name and Title
Borin Ironfist, Keeper of the Iron Throne

## Age
138 years old, his days echoing the ancient wars of his clan.

## Family
Born to the Stonehammer clan, his father was a legendary smith and his mother a warrior who never raised a single banner against kin.

## Background Story
When the iron mountains cracked open and a marauding army of orcs threatened the lowlands, young Borin was but a boy with a hammer in hand. He refused to flee, instead swinging his father’s battle‑forged axe to hold the line. That day forged his name and his destiny; he swore never to let the iron rise again.

## Personality and Character Traits
Stoic, unflinching, and fiercely loyal, Borin speaks little but his actions are thunderous. He takes pride in his strength and will protect his clan above all else.

## Occupation
Champion of the Stonehammer, mercenary warrior hired to defend frontier towns and noble houses alike.

## Abilities and Skills
Strength (18), Athletics (16), Weapon Proficiency: Hammer (1d10+5), Survival (14), Perception (13). He carries a stone‑bound shield and a massive iron hammer.

## Physical Appearance
A mountain of muscle, Borin’s beard is braided with iron filings, his skin is sun‑bronzed, and a jagged scar runs from his left cheek to his jaw—remnant of the battle that made him legend.

## Clothing
He wears battered chain‑plate armor reinforced with riveted steel plates, a heavy greaves on each foot, and a bear‑shaped shield emblazoned with the Stonehammer rune.

## Food Preferences
He devours hearty stews, roasted boar, and spiced root vegetables. Meatless days are reserved for the clan’s sacred rite of the iron moon.

## Favorite Quote
“A good hammer finds its mark.”

## A Secret word (only one)
Iron
