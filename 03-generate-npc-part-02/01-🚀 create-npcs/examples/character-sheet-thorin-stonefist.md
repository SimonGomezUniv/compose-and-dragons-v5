 # CHARACTER SHEET

## Name and Title
Thorin Stonefist

## Age
56

## Family
Thorin is the son of the late king Dain II of Erebor, a great warrior himself. His mother was Lady Thranduil, daughter of Thranduil, the last king of the Elven kingdom of Rivendell.

## Background Story
Thorin was born in the Glittering Caves of Erebor during the reign of his grandfather, Thranduil. His father, Dain II, was the greatest dwarf warrior of the time, and he taught Thorin all the skills he knows. When the dragon Smaug attacked Erebor, Thorin was just 13 years old. Together with his father and other dwarf warriors, he fought and eventually killed the dragon.

## Personality and Character Traits
Thorin is a fierce, brave, and loyal dwarf. He is known for his courage and strength, and he is always ready to defend his people and his home. He has a short temper and can be hot-headed at times, but he always means well.

## Occupation
Thorin is a warrior and a leader of the dwarf people of Erebor. He is also a skilled blacksmith and an expert in the use of weapons and armor.

## Abilities and Skills
* Warrior: Thorin is a skilled warrior with a reputation for his courage and strength.
* Blacksmith: Thorin is an expert blacksmith and can craft fine weapons and armor.
* Archery: Thorin is an expert marksman and can use a bow and arrow with great accuracy.
* Riding: Thorin is a skilled rider and can manage any horse or beast of burden.

## Physical Appearance
Thorin is a stout dwarf, with a strong, muscular build. He has short, dark hair and a beard that matches his hair. His eyes are piercing and blue, and his skin is tanned from the sun and hard work. He is always well-groomed and carries himself with dignity and pride.

## Clothing
Thorin typically wears a chain mail shirt, a short tunic, and leather armor. He carries a large, two-handed axe and wears a leather pouch on his belt to store weapons or other items. His boots are sturdy and made of leather, and he often wears a cloak made of fine wool or fur.

## Food Preferences
Thorin prefers hearty meals with meat, bread, and ale. He is not overly fond of fruit or vegetables, as he finds them to be too delicate for a dwarf.

## Favorite Quote
"A dwarf never grows old, for he was never young." - Thorin's father, Dain II
