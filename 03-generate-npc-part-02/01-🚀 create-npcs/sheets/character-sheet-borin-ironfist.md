# CHARACTER SHEET

## Name and Title
Borin Ironfist, Keeper of the Anvil

## Age
124 years

## Family
Born to a dwarf mother who was a master smith and a father who died in a tragic mine collapse, Borin was raised alone in his mother’s workshop. He has no siblings, but the memory of his father’s sacrifice fuels his devotion to protecting his craft.

## Background Story
When the mines that claimed his father fell silent, Borin swore to forge weapons that would never break. He trained relentlessly, mastering both the hammer of his mother and the steel of his own will. Now, he roams the world as a blacksmith, crafting tools of war for those who honor honor, while his unbreakable iron heart defies any foe.

## Personality and Character Traits
Borin is stoic, honorable, and fiercely loyal. He values tradition above all and will not compromise his craft for convenience. His temper is low, but when provoked, his iron fists can shatter stone.

## Occupation
Blacksmith and warrior, specializing in crafting armor and battle‑ready weapons for worthy clients.

## Abilities and Skills
- Martial Arts (Dwarven Strength)
- Shield Fighting
- Smithing (Dwarven Craft)
- Intimidation (Dwarven Pride)

## Physical Appearance
A mountain of muscle with broad, scarred forearms. His beard is thick as a dwarf’s, and a faint line runs from his temple to his jaw from a mining accident. His eyes are a deep, unblinking gray, and his skin is the warm bronze of aged iron.

## Clothing
He wears heavy dwarven chainmail reinforced with a steel breastplate, a leather surcoat over it, and thick, iron‑bound boots. A leather belt holds his hammer, the sacred anvil, and a dagger forged in his mother’s forge.

## Food Preferences
He craves hearty stews, fresh mead, and a slice of dark bread—meals that echo the depth of his iron soul. He avoids anything too light, as it reminds him of the thin walls of his childhood home.

## Favorite Quote
“Iron never lies.”

## A Secret word (only one)
Iron
